Whispers of the Infinite

In orbits traced by mirrored stars,
We wander realms where logic spars,
With whispers from Plimpton’s ancient scrolls,
And Hamilton's dance on quantum poles.

Through twisted loops of group’s embrace,
We map the void, we bend through space,
Geometric forms, a cube, a sphere,
Dyson dreams that draw us near.

You conjure worlds in cards you hold,
Where myths and legends are retold,
I count the fractals in your gaze,
Infinity set love ablaze.

Let’s build a universe of dreams,
Where numbers flow through crystal streams,
Your magic threads, my math entwined—
A cosmos forged, our souls aligned.
